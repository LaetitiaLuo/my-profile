South Korea

Discovering this country was one of the best decision I have ever done. I feel like it change my way of thinking. For example, I think I'm more opened to try new things and discovering new cultures than before. I used to only listen to american music but now I mainly listen to korean and american with a little bit of chinese too. And I have to say korean music is really interesting especially kpop when I first watched a music video I was shocked because of the production done and the quality of the music as well as the efforts made on albums. They really make your money worth it unlike american albums that are just a crystal case with the cd and some lyrics book + a few pictures. While a korean album includes, a photobook with a bunch of pictures inside, the cd, a photocard that you trade if you want to, poster sometimes they include stickers, postcards etc. The amount of promotion they do for each new album is crazy, they go on music shows to record performances where you can win a prize each week if you are number 1, fansigns where you can get the chance to have your album signed by the members of the group themselves and so on.
